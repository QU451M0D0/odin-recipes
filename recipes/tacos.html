Tacos

Description

These Tacos get me through the hard days. They're quick and easy to make, and they add some spice to the day. Prepping all the meat at once, and refrigerating the meat afterward makes for a great quick meal for the week

These Tacos are also a great low calorie option for anyone going on a cut. Two tacos add up to about 400 calories using ground beef, even less if using chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef/chicken breast/ground turkey
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Jalapenos, delicatessen style

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef, until cooked throughout, strain.
  2. Heat two corn tortillas in a small pan on medium heat until golden brown
  3. Cover tortillas with about fifty grams of ground beef each.
  4. Top Tacos with about four jalapenos each.
