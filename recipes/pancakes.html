Pancakes

Description

Fluffy Pancakes with crispy edges. Easy to make, easy to eat. My father taught me how to make these pancakes on Sunday mornings. He always makes the best pancakes. He knows a great recipe for burnt sausage too.

I love making these pancakes for Sam. She looks so cute munching and crunching on them. She likes them with Chocolate syrup and pancake syrup.

Ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Water
  • Pan or flattop grill
  • Olive oil/avocado oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the pancake mix as instructed on the box
  2. Pour about a tablespoon of oil into medium hot pan
  3. Pour pancake mix into pan until about a four inch pancake is made
  4. When bubbles appear in the pancake, flip the pancake
  5. After cooking, lob a quarter of a tablespoon of butter beside the pancake
  6. Lift the pancake to allow butter to melt underneath the pancake
  7. Allow the pancake to cook in the butter for half a minute, flip pancake and cook for another half minute
  8. Serve
