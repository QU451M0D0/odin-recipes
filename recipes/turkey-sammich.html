Turkey Sammich

Description

This is a turkey sandwich, plain and simple. Be careful, and it won't turn into a turkey SADwich.

This is one of my favorite low calorie food options for when I am cutting weight. If you use prison bread, it's only about 300 calories for one sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Prison bread
  • Sliced turkey
  • Dill pickles
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. On one slice of bread, fold four pieces of turkey slices evenly spaced
  2. Cover the sliced turkey with pickles. This is imperative to create the crunch
  3. Lightly spread mustard on a separate piece of bread
  4. Place mustard side down on top of pickle slices
  5. Cut sandwich diaganally, triangles always taste better
